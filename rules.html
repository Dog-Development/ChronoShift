Rules

Failure to obey any of these rules may result in a cancellation of your expedition, a fine upwards of $10,000, legal action taken by ChronoShift, death, serious injury, life imprisonment, or other applicable government action. Refunds will not be given to those who have had their expedition cancelled.

For All Expeditions

  1. Do not attempt to leave designated safe travel areas at any point or for any reason.
  2. Do not leave anything behind while on an expedition.
  3. Do not leave take anything while on an expedition.
  4. Obey any and all instructions from the guides or other staff.

For Hunting Expeditions

  1. Do not fire without the order from your guide.
  2. Do not attempt to take trophies from kills.
  3. All hunters must pass the required safety exam and demonstrate proper understanding of firearms safety.
